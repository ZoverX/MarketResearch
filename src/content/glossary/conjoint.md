title: Conjoint
description: A glossary entry for conjoint.
term: Conjoint
shortDefinition: A trade-off based method used to estimate the value of product features and price levels.
category: Pricing
order: 4
tags: [Pricing, Methods]
related: [attribute, utility]
---
Conjoint is used when the business decision depends on how respondents trade features and price against one another, rather than rating each one in isolation.
