title: Attribute
description: A glossary entry for attribute.
term: Attribute
shortDefinition: A feature or dimension included in a research design, especially in conjoint or choice studies.
category: Methods
order: 1
tags: [Methods]
related: [utility]
---
Attributes define what varies in an exercise or model. In conjoint they are the dimensions respondents trade off, such as price, package size, or support level.
