## Suggested workflow

1. Confirm the business question and outputs needed.
2. Review the raw file structure and metadata.
3. Clean, label, and organize variables before analysis.
4. Set weights and banner definitions early.
5. Build crosstabs, significance checks, and charts from a stable base.
6. Save reusable outputs and reporting structures.
